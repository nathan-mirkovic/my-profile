My project 💻

I wanted to create a site that could fit everyone. Especially for those who have a specific desire for what they are looking for. Thus, I created Venus, the first site with precise search criteria on physics or personality. Venus offers you the opportunity to add the person you like but also to send him a motivational message to show who you are and give you a higher possibility that he accepts you.
